Whoever is on lab-chores duty for the week looks after our plants. It takes
about five minutes.

## Watering schedule

| Plant | Where | Water |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pothos | by the window | once a week |
| Snake plant | near the door | every two weeks |
| Succulents | on the bench | every two weeks, lightly |

## Tips

- Check the top inch of soil first — if it is still damp, wait a day.
- Rotate each pot a quarter-turn so it grows evenly toward the light.
- Yellow leaves usually mean **too much** water, not too little.

Questions? Ask whoever set this up, or leave a note on the whiteboard.
